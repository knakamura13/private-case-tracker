# Tasks vs Timeline: Purpose and Usage

## Tasks

The **Tasks** route (`/tasks`) is for personal todos and errands that are **not related to legal proceedings**. This is a kanban board for tracking day-to-day items.

**Examples of Tasks:**

- Things to buy (supplies, clothing, equipment)
- Errands to run (post office, bank, appointments)
- Personal reminders (haircut, dentist, car maintenance)
- Household items (groceries, repairs, maintenance)

**Status columns:**

- To Do
- In Progress
- Done

## Timeline

The **Timeline** route (`/timeline`) is for legal proceedings and case milestones. This tracks the phases of your legal case from preparation through final outcome.

**Examples of Timeline milestones:**

- Court dates and hearings
- Filing deadlines
- Evidence submission requirements
- Attorney meetings
- Case phase transitions (Preparation, Discovery, Trial, etc.)

**Key difference:** Tasks are for personal life management, while Timeline is for legal case management. Do not mix these two purposes.
